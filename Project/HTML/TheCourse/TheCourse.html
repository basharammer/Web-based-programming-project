4-Creating Pixel Art from an Object (Sizing Tips!) When translating a reference object into pixel art, it’s important to select a sizing based on the desired level of detail, or any pre-existing size constraints for a particular purpose. In this video we’ll go through the concept of “Pixel Vision”, in order to spot the details that are important and size out suitable pixel art dimensions.
5-Hue Shifting in Pixel Art (Color Tutorial) Selecting colors for your pixel art can be tricky, but there's a fundamental principle that can give your work more vibrancy and appeal, and it's called “Hue Shifting”! In this video we break down what Hue Shifting is and how to use it to your advantage when making color selections!
6-1-Bit Pixel Art Techniques (Tutorial + Timelapse) Although 1-bit pixel art only uses two colours, don’t let its simplicity deceive you! The restriction leads to some unique approaches, and working in 1-bit is a very fun and unique challenge. In this video we explore a few ways to approach the 1-bit style, and then I share a timelapse of a brand new 1-bit pixel art piece!

8-How to "Proofread" Your Pixel Art (5 Review Tips!) When creating our own artwork from scratch, it can be difficult to assess if there are any mistakes or anything that could be done to improve the final product. In this video, I share 5 things that I do to review - or “proofread” - my own pixel art, and I hope they’ll be useful for you as well!
